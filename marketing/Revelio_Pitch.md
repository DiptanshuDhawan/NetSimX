# Revelio Labs: The Future of Network Engineering Training

## The Problem
For decades, learning network engineering (CCNA, CCNP, etc.) has been incredibly frustrating. Students and professionals are forced to spend hours fighting with clunky, outdated emulation software like GNS3, EVE-NG, or Packet Tracer just to get a lab running. 
They have to hunt down specific Cisco IOS images, configure hypervisors, manage complex bridging, and deal with mysterious VM crashes. 
By the time the lab environment is finally working, their study time is over, and their motivation is gone. Furthermore, there is no immediate feedback mechanism—students have to manually check their own configurations, never truly knowing if they solved the lab correctly.

## The Solution: Revelio Labs
Revelio Labs is a modern, browser-based network simulation platform that removes 100% of the friction from learning. 
We provide a sleek, Next.js web application where complex network topologies are spun up in seconds with a single click. 

## The Target Audience
1. IT Students studying for Cisco certifications (CCNA, CCNP).
2. Junior Network Engineers looking to practice real-world scenarios safely.
3. Corporate Training Programs that need scalable, instant lab environments for their employees.

## The Vision
We believe that infrastructure practice should be as accessible as writing code in a browser. Revelio Labs transforms network study from a painful, multi-hour chore into an instant, gamified, and highly visual experience.
